What Does it Mean to Join The Team?

Memphis Hack Club has started a robotics team! If you're interested in robotics, we'd love to have you on the team. In partnership with Memphis Robotics League, MHC Teams will be participating in a Robot Sumo Wrestling tournament!
Students will work in teams of 1-3 to design, build, and test their own robot. Multiple dates will be setup to meet in-person leading up to the tournament on April 16th. You will learn how to program and use the robots together with your team!

Supplies

MHC will be supplying the teams with their Rokit Smart Robotics Kit to use.

The kits will be provided by MHC and participation will be cost free.


Donations

While joining and participating is free to all members, donations are always appreciated.
